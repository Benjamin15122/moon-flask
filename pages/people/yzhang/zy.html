Biography

I received my B. Eng. degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering School from North China Electric Power University (Beijing) (NCEPU) in July 2008.
After that, I worked as a electrical engineer at Daya Bay NPP (DNMC) for nearly 5 years.
I was enrolled in the Graduate School of Nanjing University in Sept. 2014.
Now I am pursuing the doctoral degree under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoxing Ma and Prof. Chang Xu .

Research

My research interests are distributed systems and cloud computing.
