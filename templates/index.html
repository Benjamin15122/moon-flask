    • Introduction

      The Institute of Computer Software (ICS) is affiliated with Nanjing University and supported by the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Nanjing University. ICS was established at 1984 by Professor Jiafu Xu, the present director of ICS. Past 31 years of growing, ICS now has {{ members.faculty|length }} faculties, {{ members.phd|length}} PhD Students and {{ members.graduates|length }} graduates.

      The research interests of ICS mainly focus on Internetware-based Software Methodology, Self-Adaptive Software, Software Dynamic Evolution, Context-aware Modeling, Software Trustiness, Software Quality, Software Requirements Modeling and Analysis etc.
